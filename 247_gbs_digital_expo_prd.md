# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
## 247GBS DIGITAL EXPO

---

## 1. Product Purpose

The Digital Expo is a permanent online business environment where businesses and customers meet, interact, earn rewards, and build long-term relationships.

The Expo must:
- Allow businesses to display themselves
- Allow customers to discover businesses
- Reward every interaction
- Connect rewards, groups, wallets, and networks

The Expo must feel like:
> Walking into a global business exhibition that never closes.

---

## 2. User Types

### A. Businesses (Exhibitors)
Businesses that:
- Sell products or services
- Want customers and referrals
- Want repeat income
- Want loyalty

### B. Customers (Visitors)
Customers that:
- Want discounts and rewards
- Want to earn income
- Want community
- Want opportunities

### C. 247GBS (System Owner)
247GBS:
- Provides infrastructure
- Controls the platform
- Manages billing and compliance
- Earns from platform usage

---

## 3. Business Features (Expo Stand)

Every business must receive:

1. Business Profile Page
2. Reward Management System
3. Customer Group
4. Network Engine
5. Digital Wallet
6. QR Code
7. Customer Database
8. Referral System

These form the business digital exhibition stand.

---

## 4. Customer Features

Every customer must receive:

1. Personal Wallet
2. Reward Account
3. User Profile
4. Personal QR Code
5. Network Dashboard
6. Joined Business List
7. Group Access

Customers are members of the Expo economy.

---

## 6. Extended Expo Platform Systems (Operational & Management Layer)

To ensure the Digital Expo operates as a complete commercial ecosystem, the following systems must be implemented.

### A. Exhibitor Portal (Business Backend)

This is the main control panel for businesses.

It must allow exhibitors to:
- Register and verify accounts
- Choose and pay for Expo packages
- Upload and manage booth content
- View orders and bookings
- Manage rewards and groups
- Track earnings
- Access support

---

### B. Booth Builder & Templates

A simple setup wizard must allow businesses to:
- Upload logo and banners
- Add company description
- List products and services
- Upload videos and images
- Schedule live sessions
- Add contact details
- Preview their booth

Templates must be available for different business types.

---

### C. Product & Service Listing Sync

The system must:
- Import listings from MCOM Mall
- Allow manual product creation
- Sync pricing and availability
- Support digital and physical products
- Support service listings

---

### D. Payment Gateway & Split Payout System

The payment system must:
- Accept cards, transfers, and wallets
- Support multi-currency
- Split payments automatically
- Hold funds in escrow (when required)
- Release payouts based on rules
- Generate transaction records

---

### E. Live Streaming & Webinar Integration

Businesses must be able to:
- Host live product demos
- Run Q&A sessions
- Organise webinars
- Embed Zoom, YouTube Live, or internal players
- Schedule sessions with reminders

---

### F. Chat & Lead Capture System

Each booth must include:
- Live chat
- Automated replies
- Lead capture forms
- Email and phone collection
- CRM integration

All leads must be saved in the business dashboard.

---

### G. Booking & Appointment System

For service providers, the platform must:
- Allow slot creation
- Display availability
- Accept bookings
- Collect payments
- Send confirmations
- Handle cancellations

---

### H. Order & Fulfilment Management

Businesses must be able to:
- View orders
- Process payments
- Manage delivery status
- Upload tracking numbers
- Handle refunds and returns
- Deliver digital products

---

### I. Support & Moderation Tools

Admins must be able to:
- Approve or reject booths
- Remove harmful content
- Suspend accounts
- Respond to complaints
- Review transactions
- Enforce platform rules

---

### J. Marketing & Promotion Toolkit

The system must provide:
- Email templates
- Banner templates
- Social media designs
- Referral links
- Promo codes
- Campaign tracking tools

---

### K. Analytics & Reporting Dashboard

Analytics must show:
- Page views
- Clicks
- Chat activity
- Sales volume
- Conversion rates
- Reward performance
- Network growth

Reports must be exportable.

---

### L. Email & SMS Automation System

The communication system must send:
- Registration confirmations
- Payment receipts
- Session reminders
- Booking alerts
- Abandoned cart reminders
- Reward notifications

---

### M. Media Hosting & Performance System

The platform must include:
- Secure image hosting
- Video streaming support
- CDN integration
- Automatic optimisation
- Backup storage

This ensures fast loading worldwide.

---

### N. Security, Compliance & Governance

The system must support:
- SSL encryption
- Data protection
- KYC for sellers (when required)
- Prohibited item controls
- Tax configuration
- Audit logs
- Fraud monitoring

---

### O. Central Admin Dashboard

The master admin panel must allow:
- Event creation and management
- Package pricing setup
- Seller approval
- Refund processing
- System monitoring
- User management
- Report exports

---

### P. Support Documentation & Training System

The platform must provide:
- Step-by-step guides
- Video tutorials
- FAQ system
- Staff manuals
- Business onboarding courses
